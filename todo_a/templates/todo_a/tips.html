Here is some tips for using this application

  1. Click your name and it will take you to the homepage.
  2. You can do two things:
    1. Make and track your event list.
    2. Fix your budget and track the money, "spend or earned"
  3. In the todo app you have 3 options:
    1. Create : You can create a new event with title, memo and you can mark this event as important
    2. Current : You can see your current todos. Important ones will be highlighted. By clicking an event you can edit, mark as completed and delete the event
    3. Completed : You can see the completed events and the time when completed then you can delete the event.
  4. In the CashCount app you have 3 options:
    1. Create : You can create a new Cash Memo with title, memo, amount and mark if the money taken from you or given to you
    2. ShowMemos : You can see your current memos. Also you can see total money taken from you or given to you. In the memo list you also can see the time when that memo was created. By clicking an event you can edit and delete the Memo.
    3. Budget : Now this is where your total budget is fixed and updated whenever a memo is created.
  5. You can interchange between these two application by clicking TODOS or CashCount button.
